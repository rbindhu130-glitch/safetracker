About SafeTracker

SafeTracker is a safety-focused platform designed to help people feel secure while traveling on roads and in public places.

Many women, students, and elderly people face fear due to dark streets, lack of support, and unsafe environments. SafeTracker connects users with verified volunteers for real-time assistance.

Our mission is to build a safer, more aware community where everyone can move freely without fear.
